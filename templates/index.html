Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Subsitech and how we help you find funding.

How do I know if I'm eligible for a scheme?

You can use our built-in Eligibility Checker tool! By entering your academic and financial details, our system automatically matches you with schemes you qualify for.

Is Subsitech free for students?

Yes, searching for schemes and using our eligibility tool is completely free for all students. We are committed to making financial aid accessible to everyone.

How do I contact a donor directly?

Once you've submitted an application, you can message donors through our secure portal to ask clarifying questions or provide additional updates.

What documents do I need to prepare?

Commonly required documents include institutional ID, transcripts, income certificates, and personal statements. Each scheme will list its specific requirements in the application form.
