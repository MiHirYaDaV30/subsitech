Privacy Policy

How Subsitech collects, stores, and protects user information.

Data Protection

We store only the information needed to match applicants with schemes, process registrations, and manage application history. Sensitive details are handled with limited access controls and protected application sessions.

Information We Use

Account profile details, application form responses, and eligibility answers are used to personalize scheme recommendations and support reporting for donors.

Questions

Privacy questions can be sent to `privacy@subsitech.org`.
